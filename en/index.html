Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does COLOBUS work?

COLOBUS is based on algorithms from quantitative value investing. Every week, we analyze the financial data of thousands of companies in an automated fashion and derive stock recommendations. Our strategies are validated against up to 20 years of stock market history (so-called “backtesting”).

How much money can I invest with COLOBUS?

As much as you like. We recommend a minimum amount of 8,000€ to minimize the effect of trading fees. Because our strategies also contain small cap stocks, you should beware that investing large sums (500,000€+) may potentially expose you to liquidity issues (ask-bid-spread).

How much does COLOBUS cost?

We charge a fixed annual fee of 399€ regardless of whether you invest 10,000€ or 100,000€. If you buy a multi-year package (which we recommend) the annual fee is reduced by 16% or even 24%.

Does COLOBUS manage my money?

No. COLOBUS is an investment service that sends you buy/sell recommendations for European stocks for a fixed annual fee. You remain in charge of your money and manage your trading account as you wish.
